Studio Process

From Concept Sketch to Qalam on Paper

Each artwork is developed through iterative composition studies before final hand lettering on prepared paper.

Artist Statement

Tradition in Dialogue With Contemporary Form

My artistic process is fueled by the challenge of creativity within the disciplined structure of Arabic and Islamic calligraphy.

Before using the qalam on paper, I develop ideas digitally, then refine final script by hand.
