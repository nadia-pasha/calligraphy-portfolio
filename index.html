Artist Statement

Writing With Breath, Pressure, and Pace

I treat each line as a record of movement. My work blends historical calligraphic forms with contemporary spacing, creating pieces that feel both archival and immediate. I am especially drawn to the emotional weight of negative space.

Most pieces are made with broad-edge nibs, walnut ink, and handmade paper. The final compositions are then adapted for editorial layouts, gallery prints, and private commissions.
